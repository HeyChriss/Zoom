About Zoom

Zoom is BYU-Idaho's new vehicle for audio and video conferencing.

Zoom capabilities include:

  • Integrating with Outlook Calendar
  • Free, unlimited usage
  • Works on any device
  • Screensharing and whiteboard
  • Recording your meetings
  • Invite up to 300 participants
